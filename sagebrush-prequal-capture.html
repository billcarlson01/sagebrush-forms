Property Qualification Script ▾
Ask this "Can I get the address of the property? And just to confirm — you're the homeowner, not renting?" — pause — "And do you have an HOA? Sometimes they have approval requirements for solar."
⛔ Renter — cannot proceed. Politely explain solar requires homeowner approval. Log as Disqualified in HubSpot.

Financial Fit Script ▾
Ask this "What's your average NV Energy bill — roughly? Just a ballpark is fine." — pause — "And are you thinking cash purchase, or would financing options be helpful to look at?"
⚠ Low bill — ROI math may be marginal. Still proceed to survey but set expectations: payback could be 12–15 years at this usage level.
4
System Interest Script ▾
Ask this "Are you looking at just solar panels, or also battery backup? We install Tesla Powerwall and Enphase batteries. And do you have an EV or are you thinking about one?" — pause — "Any goals beyond the bill — backup power, going fully off-grid?"
5
Timeline & Decision Script ▾
Ask this "Are you looking to move forward in the next 30, 60, or 90 days — or is this more exploratory right now?" — pause — "And is there anyone else involved in the decision — a spouse or partner you'd want on the call when we go over the proposal?"

01 ★ Lead Capture HubSpot ▼

Every inbound inquiry (web, phone, referral, canvass) gets a HubSpot contact within 15 minutes. Speed-to-lead is the single biggest driver of close rate in residential solar.

  • ◆ Create Contact in HubSpot — First/Last, Phone, Email, Source
  • ◆ Set Deal Stage: Lead Capture in Solar Ops Clean pipeline
  • ◆ Log every prior touchpoint as Activities (web form, emails, calls)
  • ◆ Set a follow-up Task: call within 2 hrs of inbound, or same day for after-hours
  • ◆ Tag lead source (Website, Referral, Canvass, Repeat) for pipeline reporting
HubSpot fields to populate: Contact Owner · Lead Source · Phone (landline vs cell) · First Contact Date · Notes (verbatim from voicemail/form)
02 Pre-Qualification Call HubSpot ▼

15–20 minute call. Goal: confirm fit, gather design inputs, set next step. Don't sell — qualify. A bad lead that makes it to proposal costs you 4+ hours.

  • ◆ Property address + confirm homeowner (not renter)
  • ◆ Average monthly NV Energy bill (target: $150+/mo for strong ROI)
  • ◆ Roof age and type (shingle, tile, metal) — HOA restrictions?
  • ◆ Interest in storage (Powerwall)? EV charger add-on?
  • ◆ Decision-maker status — spouse/partner need to be on call?
  • ◆ Timeline: "Are you looking to move forward in the next 30/60/90 days?"
  • ◆ Schedule site survey before hanging up — offer 2 specific times
Update Deal: Stage → Pre-Qualification · Add: Address, Monthly Bill, Roof Type, Storage Interest, Survey Date scheduled · Log full call notes
03 Site Survey HubSpot + Drive ▼

On-site visit to capture roof dimensions, shading, main panel specs, and utility meter. This data feeds directly into the Enphase/Aurora system design.

  • ◆ Create Google Drive project folder: WASHOE-[YR]-Davis-[Address]
  • ◆ Paste Drive folder URL into HubSpot Deal → Project Folder URL field
  • ◆ Capture: roof pitch, azimuth, sq ft per plane, shading obstacles
  • ◆ Main panel: brand, amperage (200A upgrade needed?), meter location
  • ◆ Photos: all roof planes, panel, meter, attic access, structural concerns
  • ◆ Upload photos to Drive folder → 01-SiteSurvey subfolder
  • ◆ Note any structural, HOA, or AHJ flags
Drive folder structure: 01-SiteSurvey / 02-Design / 03-Permit / 04-Install / 05-Commissioning / 06-Closeout
04 Proposal HubSpot + Drive ▼

Deliver a written proposal within 48 hours of site survey. Include system design, production estimates, pricing (cash/loan/PPA), and ITC details. Walk it in person or via video call — never email-only.

  • ◆ Complete Enphase Installer Platform / Aurora system design
  • ◆ Calculate kWh offset (target 90–100% of annual usage)
  • ◆ 3 options: cash, Mosaic/GoodLeap loan, and (if applicable) Powerwall bundle
  • ◆ Include 30% federal ITC + any NV state incentives
  • ◆ NEM 3.0 / NV Energy net metering rate schedule — include payback timeline
  • ◆ Save proposal PDF to Drive → 02-Design
  • ◆ Log proposal amount in HubSpot Deal Value field
  • ◆ Set follow-up Task: 48hr check-in call after proposal delivery
HubSpot: Stage → Proposal Delivered · Deal Amount = proposed system price · Proposal Date · Follow-up Task created
05 Contract + Deposit HubSpot + Drive ▼

Nevada C-2 contractor requirements apply. Use a written contract with scope, payment schedule, permit disclosure, and 3-day right of rescission notice per NRS 598.974.

  • ◆ DocuSign or wet-signed contract with full scope of work
  • ◆ Payment schedule: typically 10% deposit · 40% permit approval · 40% install · 10% commissioning
  • ◆ Include NV 3-day right of rescission notice (mandatory)
  • ◆ Collect deposit check or ACH — issue receipt immediately
  • ◆ Save signed contract to Drive → 06-Closeout/Contracts
  • ◆ Log deposit received in HubSpot · Stage → Contract Signed
  • ◆ Begin permit package preparation
Note: Per Sciascia situation — ensure payment schedule milestones are crystal clear in writing before any work begins.
06 Pre-Installation HubSpot + Drive ▼

Permit, utility interconnection application, material procurement. This stage takes 2–6 weeks in Washoe County. Keep the customer informed weekly — silence kills trust.

  • ◆ Submit permit to Washoe County Building / City of Reno — upload to Drive 03-Permit
  • ◆ Submit NV Energy interconnection application (PTO required pre-energize)
  • ◆ Order equipment: Enphase microinverters, modules, Powerwall (if applicable)
  • ◆ Schedule install crew and crane/lift if needed
  • ◆ Weekly customer update — email or text with permit status
  • ◆ Log permit approval date in HubSpot · Collect milestone payment
HubSpot: Stage → Pre-Installation · Log permit # · AHJ name · Expected install date
07 Installation Field + Drive ▼

1–2 day install. C-2 license work — document everything. Inspect at completion before crew leaves site.

  • ◆ Pre-install customer call day before: confirm access, pets, Wi-Fi password for Enphase gateway
  • ◆ Installation photos: each row of panels, all wire runs, combiner box, inverters
  • ◆ Enphase gateway commissioned on-site — confirm all modules reporting
  • ◆ Panel upgrade documented if applicable (before/after photos)
  • ◆ Upload all install photos to Drive → 04-Install
  • ◆ Request AHJ inspection appointment
  • ◆ Log install complete date in HubSpot · Collect milestone payment
08 Commissioning HubSpot + Drive ▼

AHJ inspection → NV Energy PTO → system live. This is the emotional peak for the homeowner — make it a moment.

  • ◆ Pass AHJ inspection — upload inspection card to Drive → 05-Commissioning
  • ◆ Submit PTO request to NV Energy with final inspection sign-off
  • ◆ Receive PTO letter — system can be energized
  • ◆ On-site commissioning visit: walk customer through Enphase app, explain production monitoring
  • ◆ Review system performance expectations and net metering billing cycle
  • ◆ Present customer with commissioning packet: warranty docs, permit card, PTO letter
  • ◆ Upload commissioning packet to Drive → 05-Commissioning
  • ◆ Invoice final payment · Stage → Commissioning Complete

09 Final Payment HubSpot ▼

Collect final payment at or immediately after commissioning. Do not leave the site without confirming payment timeline if not collected in person.

  • ◆ Issue final invoice at commissioning visit — hand-deliver or send DocuSign
  • ◆ Payment due: Net 5 from PTO date (specify in contract)
  • ◆ Log payment received in HubSpot · Mark Deal as Closed Won
  • ◆ File lien waiver upon receipt of final payment
  • ◆ Provide customer with copy of all warranty documentation
Dispute prevention: Contract language should specify "Final payment due upon system commissioning and PTO receipt" — not upon customer satisfaction review.
10 Follow-Up + Referral HubSpot ▼

The best lead is a referral. A happy solar customer talks to 5–7 neighbors in the first month. Build the ask into your process.

  • ◆ 30-day check-in call: "How's the system performing? Any questions on the app?"
  • ◆ Request Google review — send direct link via text
  • ◆ Ask directly: "Do you have any neighbors or friends who've mentioned solar? I'd love a warm intro."
  • ◆ Log referral contacts in HubSpot — create new Deals with source = Referral + original customer name
  • ◆ First-year anniversary email: system production report + referral reminder
  • ◆ Tag customer as Referral Source in HubSpot if they send leads
Referral incentive: Consider a $250–500 bill credit or gift card per installed referral — document in a simple referral program one-pager.
